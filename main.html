Mahabaleshwar

Popular Hill Station in Maharashtra

Mahabaleshwar is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra. Known for its cool climate, lush greenery, and stunning viewpoints, it is a favorite getaway for nature lovers, honeymooners, and families. The town is also famous for its juicy strawberries, ancient temples, and dense forests. With a mix of natural beauty and colonial charm, Mahabaleshwar offers a refreshing escape from the hustle of city life.


  1. Venna Lake A serene and scenic lake surrounded by greenery, where visitors can enjoy boating, horse riding, and local snacks. It’s perfect for a relaxing evening with family or friends.
  2. Arthur’s Seat Known as the "Queen of all Points," Arthur’s Seat offers breathtaking views of the valleys and the Savitri River. It's a great spot for photography and sunset watching.
  3. Mapro Garden A popular tourist spot famous for strawberry-based products like syrups, jams, and ice creams. Visitors can enjoy a factory tour, shopping, and delicious food at the garden cafe.
  4. Lingmala Waterfall A beautiful waterfall located on the way to Panchgani. The falls drop from a height of around 500 feet, creating a majestic view, especially during monsoon. Great for photography and enjoying nature's raw beauty (though swimming is not allowed)
  5. Mahabaleshwar Temple An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Old Mahabaleshwar. It houses a swayambhu (self-originated) Shiva lingam and is a key pilgrimage spot. The temple is over 500 years old and reflects the spiritual heritage of the town.
  • Pav Bhaji A spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttery pav (bread rolls). Originated in Mumbai, it’s a popular street food known for its bold flavors. Often garnished with chopped onions, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon.
  • Biryani A fragrant rice dish made with long-grain basmati rice, spices, and meat (or vegetables). Famous types include Hyderabadi Biryani and Lucknowi Biryani. Known for its rich aroma and layered cooking technique.
  • Strawberry Cream A signature dessert in Mahabaleshwar, made with fresh strawberries and whipped cream. Refreshing and sweet!
